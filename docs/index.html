Compile, Watch and Execute

The complete Kerboscript toolbox at your fingertips, use ksc to compile, watch or execute your scripts directly in your command line.

Integrated Deployment

Deploy your project to your Kerbal Space Program installation of choice with just a single command.

Custom Scripts

Since ksc simulates the kOS volume system in your command line it can be used to write custom compilation and deployment scripts with Kerboscript.
